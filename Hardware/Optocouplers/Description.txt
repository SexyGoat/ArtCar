The input side expects 5V. Each input is independent and may be
wired to have a common +5V or a common ground if desired.

The output side expects 24V and Ground in the top right dual screw
connector. The optocoulers switch high (PNP fashion) to the lower
right dual screw connector.

Diagnostic LEDs may be stuffed into the 2-hole machined sockets.
Check the polarity

PC817 (4-pin) or 4N35 (6-pin) optocouplers may be used. Set the
jump adjacent to the input side of each optocoupler appropriately.
